Get To Know Us.

We are a team of Venezuelan mountaineers with the goal of inspiring others to achieve their dreams through the achievement of ours.

Manuel Herrera

International Chef and outdoor lover. Actually studying Administration and Finances at the Rey Juan Carlos University of Madrid. Born in Barquisimeto, on the 23th October of 1995.

Alejandro Angeli

Bachelor of Mathematics and Statistics, he's about to begin his Master in Quantitative Finances al the ETH Zurich, Switzerland. Mountain pupil since a relly early age and endurance Athlete. Also born in Caracas on the 28th March of 1996.

Andres Ramos

Qualified mountain rescuer and vertical worker. Graduated as Chef from the Caracas Culinary Institut. Student of administration at the Metropolitan University of Venezuela. Currently living in Spain. Born in Caracas on July 20th 1995.

Marcos Carbonell

Programmer and mountaineer. Ex-member of the Central University of Venezuela Athletics Team. Currently studying Informatics at the Ruprecht-Karls-University of Heidelberg. Also born in Caracas on 20th May 1998.
